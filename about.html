About Dasanayake Jewellery

Crafting elegance and trust for generations

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Dasanayake Jewellery has been a trusted name in fine jewellery for over three decades. From humble beginnings in a small workshop, we have grown into one of Sri Lanka’s most recognized jewellers. Our mission is to bring timeless elegance and modern craftsmanship together, offering our customers the perfect blend of tradition and luxury.

Our Vision

We believe that jewellery is not just an accessory — it’s a reflection of love, pride, and memories. Each piece is designed with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring every customer receives something truly special. We are committed to maintaining our reputation for honesty, quality, and elegance in every creation.
